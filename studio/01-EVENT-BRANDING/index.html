Event Branding

Something I’ve always been interested in in general is branding and identity design. After some consideration, I decided to focus my theme specifically on event branding, because it comprises of many different components and allows for the chance to design a wide variety of things. Event branding is more about “creating a holistic identity built around more abstract and experiential concepts” and having a sense of continuity and adaptability in the designs. What I’m most intrigued by is the idea of creating an identity system and being able to design a full experience. For example, not only coming up with a logo and concept for something, but also designing advertising material (posters/pamphlets), merchandise, way finding systems, signage, websites etc… I’m very interested in conceptualizing and communicating ideas through visual means and the diversity of the types of events opens up a huge range of creative possibilities. Each event has a different message behind it, a different tone to it and hence requires a different way of approaching. Coming up with a method to best convey this whether its through the color palette, logo, typography, illustrations, marketing tools and/or visual style is something I’m very interested in exploring. For my research, I looked at some case studies that broke down the entire design process of coming up with an identity system and the different steps involved. What I want to explore further is the idea of creating a holistic identity and how to develop a successful identity system and style guides.
