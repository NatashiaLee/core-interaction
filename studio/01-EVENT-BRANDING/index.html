Event Branding

Something I’m quite interested in general is branding and identity design. Specifically, I decided to focus my theme on event branding, because it comprises of many different components and allows for the chance to design a wide variety of things. What I’m most intrigued by is the idea of creating an identity system and using it to create a full experience. For example, not only coming up with a logo and concept for something, but also designing advertising material, merchandise, way finding systems, websites etc…
